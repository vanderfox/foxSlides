What is ChatOps?

  • Leveraging chat software to support work flows
  • Communicating with coworkers as well as the environment

Why ChatOps?

  • Increased understanding
  • Maintain history
  • Increased collaboration
  • Increased awareness
  • Lower costs
  • Increased Quality
  • Repeatility
  • Teach by doing
